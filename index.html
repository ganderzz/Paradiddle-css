Grids

Grids in Paradiddle are based off of Flexbox. This makes responsiveness and layouts expremely easy.
All columns must be contained inside of a .row container. By default, there are 12 usable columns in a row. Editing the variables, and recompiling the sass files can give more or less columns per row.
